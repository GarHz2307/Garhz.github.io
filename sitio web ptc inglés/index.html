What is RGS (Residential Guardian System)

Our country is considered one of the more insecure countries with high ratings of mortality, people doesn't feel comfortable and secure when they go outside or even leaving their homes alone, they don't feel secure for fearing the worst, the absence of their possessions. By this fact, we have decided to create a system for security purposes.

This security system has the purpose of making a residence a safety zone with a lower price than a residence is found on an actual safety zone. The system has been planned to be easily managed by security guards. With that purpose, the system counts with the accesibility to security cameras and forms to register upcomming visitors and residents.
